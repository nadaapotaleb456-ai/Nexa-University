Program Overview

The Bachelor of Science in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Nexa University is a five-year, research-intensive program designed to develop future processor architects, systems researchers, and scientific innovators.

The curriculum emphasizes mathematical rigor, physical understanding of computation, and systems-level thinking. Course content, instructional materials, and assessments are accessible only to admitted students, while the curriculum structure is publicly visible for transparency.
