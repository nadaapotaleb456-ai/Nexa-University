# Admissions Requirements – Nexa University

Nexa University seeks exceptional students with a strong foundation in mathematics and science, demonstrating the potential for advanced research and original contribution. Admission is selective and based on both academic merit and demonstrated motivation for independent learning.

**Minimum Requirements:**
- Completion of a recognized secondary education or equivalent diploma.
- High academic achievement: minimum GPA of 3.8 (on a 4.0 scale) or equivalent.
- Demonstrated proficiency in core subjects:
  - Mathematics: Algebra, Geometry, Pre-Calculus
  - Physics: Mechanics, Electricity & Magnetism basics
  - Computer Science: Basic programming knowledge
- Evidence of initiative and research aptitude through projects, competitions, or personal work.

**Application Materials:**
- Academic transcripts and certificates
- Personal statement (max 500 words) detailing research interests and career goals
- Letters of recommendation (preferably from teachers or mentors familiar with the applicant’s academic ability)
- Optional portfolio of projects, publications, or relevant accomplishments

**Evaluation Process:**
- Applications are reviewed holistically, emphasizing readiness for accelerated, research-focused study.
- Candidates may be invited for an interview to assess critical thinking, problem-solving ability, and research potential.

Nexa University does not award admission based solely on standardized tests; demonstrated understanding, creativity, and capacity for self-directed learning are paramount. Admission is merit-based and competitive.
